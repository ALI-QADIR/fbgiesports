About Us

We are the F.B.G aka Federal Bureau of Gaming.
We are an Esports organization that aims to provide a competitive and fun environment for players in India by conducting tournaments (pay to enter and/or free entry) with cash prizes for games such as CODM, BGMI, and many more to come. (solely PC and Mobile Based).

Our vision: To strengthen and raise the status of Esports culture in India.
